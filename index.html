YOU ARE INVITED TO THE NEW F-CAMP

F-Camp has always been considered one of the most memorable events for every FPT student, a brilliant youth memory. Last year, F-Camp 2020 appeared more unique with a 10-year milestone - marking the maturity of many generations of students. Every year, when freshmen rush to school to start their study program, F-Camp kicks in. This is also a gift from previous students - under the companionship and advisor of the Department of International Cooperation and Personal Development (IC-PDP) to students of the new course at Hoa Lac Campus. Amidst the multitude of university programs to welcome new students, F-Camp is considered one of the most unique events. When the new students are asked to roll off, do their best right in the "welcome ceremony". Through a series of exciting activities that take place throughout the day, F-Camp contributes to connecting, helping new students get acquainted, learn about culture and can quickly integrate into work and study as well as life in Hoa Lac.




LIFE ON CAMPUS


7.30: Let's get it!

Students spend 2-3 periods in class, each class lasts 90 minutes, starting at 7:30 and ending at 17:20 daily. Apart from that, there is plenty of time for self-study and group projects. These activities can take place in anywhere on campus.



11.00 - 13.00: Lunch break

Normally, FPT University students will finish their main course at around 5:20 pm. After this time, is the time that students can spend in physical training and sports activities and free activities on campus. Students can choose their favorite sports like soccer, table tennis, roller skating, basketball,…. In addition, students also enjoy free use of the gym with full modern equipment, artificial ice rink, Vovinam martial arts house ... or group activities at Street workout, dance practice ground,…



18.00: The most exciting time of the day

This is time for self-studying. Normally, if students join clubs, they will spend 2 hours/session/week to participate in club activities. Hanoi FPT University currently has nearly 40 different clubs operating in many areas. Each student can choose to join many clubs depending on their interests. After a long day, students can take a walk around campus, enjoy some fresh air before getting back to the dormitory and preparing for a new day of learning.


Facilities


Each dorm room with 3-6 students has its own toilet area, equipped with a heater. In student dormitories, there is also a fireproof system and cameras along the corridor to ensure dormitory security.



Clubs at FPT University can help you integrate into the community, meet people with similar interests and improve the quality of your university life. The roles in different clubs can help you learn and practice many valuable skills in learning, communication, physical training and culture.
